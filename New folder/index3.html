Frequently asked questions

The cost of drywall repair can vary depending on the size and complexity of the repair. On average, you can expect to pay between $50 to $75 per hour for labor, plus the cost of materials.

The time it takes to repair drywall depends on the extent of the damage. Small repairs can take a few hours, while larger repairs may take a full day or more.

It is generally better to repair drywall if the damage is minor. However, if the damage is extensive, it may be more cost-effective to replace the drywall.

Drywall can crumble due to moisture, age, or poor installation. It's important to address the underlying cause to prevent further damage.
